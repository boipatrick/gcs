About Us

What we do

Gravik Climate Solutions is a non-governmental not-for-profit organization that operates in Kenya.

Our mission

The mission of Gravik Climate Solutions is to combine technology and engineering with the human element to combat the climate change issue through programs that target both urban and rural populations in the developing world.

Our Objectives

  • Discover and develop 100 climate-related solutions from university students the first year of operation
  • Collect more than 500 tons of food waste in the first year, and increase the amount collected by 20% each year.
  • Effectively distribute organic fertilizer to low-income families in arid and semi-arid areas
