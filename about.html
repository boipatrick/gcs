About Us

Gravik Climate Solutions is a non-governmental not-for-profit organization that operates in Kenya. The organization’s goal is to be at the forefront in the fight against climate change by spreading awareness on the matter and engaging in direct solutions to lower carbon gas emissions. Our Programs include

Climate Awareness Program

For the climate awareness program, approximately 70% of the country’s population qualifies for assistance from the organization. Our client base is children and young people ranging from the ages of 5-35 who represent the next generation. This generation will face some tough periods in the coming years and have to develop solutions early on. The program will use technology- based tools combined with a human touch for maximum reach.

Talent Drive Program

The talent drive program targets university students who come up with technology-based solutions to climate change but never get a voice or avenue to develop them into useful solutions.

Waste Food Program

Our third program focuses on urban families and households. As cities and towns become more crowded, so does the amount of food waste ending up in landfills, releasing harmful methane gas. We aim to collect this food waste before it reaches landfills and transform it into organic fertilizer through a process called composting. This process utilizes specialized composting machines, hence the need for an engineering division. The resulting organic fertilizer is then distributed to arid and semi-arid areas, enhancing food security in the country and region.
